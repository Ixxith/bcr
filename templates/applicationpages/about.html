Our Mission

To help minority groups everywhere find a job and company they love

Our Values

We are transparent. We are open and honest. We share information – the good and the bad – so we can continuously learn, collaborate and make the right decisions.

We are ingenious. We actively pursue new and different ways to improve job seekers chances of finding their dream job. We challenge the status quo and believe in succeeding expectations

About Us

Currently, no career platform meets the needs of minority groups looking for a career. The proposal for Black Cyber Recruiter, by our sponsor Christabel Monica Ugbang Agbonkonkon is groundbreaking and will be a pivotal force that will change the lives of thousands who come from minority groups and as such have a general disadvantage. Black Cyber Recruiter is a platform that not only helps companies to find minority applicants to apply, but focuses on the applicant and enhancing their experience. Black Cyber Recruiter is a one stop location for Black job seekers because the process to apply for jobs is as quick and easy as it can be by providing a valuable and easy to use solution to bridge the gap to help to eliminate job opportunity discrimination.
