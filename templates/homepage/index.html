We make finding a new job a breeze

Everyone deserves a job they love

No matter the background, we want you to be able to find a job that you love to come to. We use machine learning to help recommend jobs that are most likely to suit your goals. Furthermore we work with employers to set standards that are more accurate indicators of performance rather than the historic standards often used on other websites, such as GPAs.


Never miss an application again!

We all know the daily rush keeps us busy and usually applying for jobs is the last thing on our minds. Our website combats this, using a calender to track application deadlines, customised newsletters to keep you up to date on possible job oppertunities, and can even apply to jobs automatically in your behalf, using a provided resume.


All job applications are straight to the point

No one likes to apply for a job where they don't even know how much they will make. This is why we require all job applications to post the salary/wages, include any other compensations, and list if they will provide relocation assistance.
